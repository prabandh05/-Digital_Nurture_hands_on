Community Bulletin

The city council invites residents to participate in the monthly neighborhood forum. Updates include road safety work, park maintenance, library programs, and new volunteering opportunities. Residents can submit questions before the meeting or speak directly during the open discussion session.

Local clubs are also coordinating cultural performances, weekend reading circles, and sports coaching for students. Notices will be refreshed every Friday at the ward office and on the community portal.
